Strategy 1: Maximizing Language Exposure

What does it mean?

Immerse yourself in the language through daily life activities and combine listening, watching, and reading for a multi-sensory experience

🎬 Watching movies & series
📚 Reading books, articles, blogs
🎵 Listening to music, podcasts, etc.

Comprehensive Input Theory

Theory by Stephen Krashen: Comprehensible input is crucial for language acquisition.


The more you hear and read in your target language, the more you understand and learn—almost effortlessly.

My Implementation Plan

🎬 Movies/Series

Watch 3 episodes per week with subtitles to help understand new phrases and accents

📚 Reading

Read one article or book chapter daily, choosing topics that genuinely interest me

🎵 Listening

Listen to podcasts or music during commute and chores—turning "dead time" into practice

Netflix
YouTube
Spotify
News Apps

Measuring Progress

  • Track new words and expressions in a notebook or app
  • Test comprehension by summarizing what I've watched, read, or listened to
  • Notice improvements in listening and reading skills over time

Strategy 2: Keeping a Journal

Why a journal?

✍️ Boosts writing skills and self-expression
🤔 Encourages reflection on daily life in the target language
🎯 Builds confidence and fluency

My Journal Plan

📝 Daily Writing

Write 5-10 sentences every evening about my day, what I learned, or how I felt

📅 Weekly Review

Review and correct entries weekly, looking for patterns and improvements

Paper notebook
Google Docs
Journaling app

Measuring Journal Progress

  • Compare first and latest entries monthly
  • Look for longer sentences and richer vocabulary
  • Track fewer mistakes over time
  • Notice improved confidence in self-expression

Expected Benefits

Strategy 1: Language Exposure

Natural, enjoyable exposure leads to better comprehension, expanded vocabulary, and improved listening skills

Strategy 2: Journal Writing

Enhanced writing ability, increased confidence, and improved capacity to think in the target language

Conclusion

  • Combining exposure and active practice = balanced language growth
  • Consistency and reflection will help me reach my goals
  • These strategies make learning enjoyable, effective, and sustainable
